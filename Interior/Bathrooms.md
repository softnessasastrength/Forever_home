**Aesthetic:** Warm, welcoming, nostalgic — whimsical grandma home  
**Status:** Concept + schematic specs locked for Revit  
**Revit-ready specs:** [Architecture/Bathrooms_Specs.md](../Architecture/Bathrooms_Specs.md)  
**Renders:** [Master](../Renderings/Bathroom_Master_Concept.jpg) · [Jack & Jill](../Renderings/Bathroom_Jack_and_Jill_Concept.jpg)

## Intent

Bathrooms should feel like the rest of the house: soft light, cream millwork, familiar traditional fixtures — never a cold hotel spa or all-gray showroom. The **primary bath** is a private retreat. The **Jack & Jill** is deliberately **open and spacious** so two secondary bedrooms share a full bath without feeling like a closet between doors.

## Primary (master) full bath

| Item | Direction |
|---|---|
| Size | **9'-0" × 10'-0"** (90 sf) |
| Access | Door from **primary bedroom only** |
| Vanity | **60"** furniture-style or shaker; cream; quartz; aged brass; 1–2 sinks |
| Toilet | Two-piece, elongated, **comfort height**, white, soft-close |
| Bathing | **60" tub/shower** alcove baseline (shower-only alternate) |
| Mood | Soft cream tile, warm metals, sconce light at mirror |

## Jack & Jill full bath

| Item | Direction |
|---|---|
| Size | **10'-0" × 12'-0"** (120 sf) — open volume |
| Access | Door from **Bedroom A** (Branden hybrid) + **Bedroom B** (guest/third) |
| Vanity | **72"** dual-sink shaker; cream; quartz; aged brass |
| Toilet | Same style as primary; optional 42" half-wall screen |
| Bathing | Large **walk-in shower** (60"×36" preferred), glass enclosure |
| Mood | Spacious floor, dual mirrors/sconces, shared linen storage |

### Open & spacious strategies

- One continuous room instead of three tiny locked compartments  
- Dual vanity on a single wall to free the center  
- Glass shower instead of opaque tub closet  
- 9'-0" ceiling; light tile; 2700K lighting  

## Finish language (both)

- Floors: warm porcelain / soft hex — not cool gray  
- Walls: cream paint + subway wet walls  
- Counters: warm quartz (kitchen family)  
- Metals: aged brass (or polished nickel house-wide)  
- Lighting: layered, dimmable, warm
